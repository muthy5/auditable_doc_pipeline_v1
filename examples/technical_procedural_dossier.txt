Title: Reactor startup checklist
Objective: Bring Reactor Unit 3 online after maintenance.
Inputs:
- Coolant loop pressure readings
- Control rod calibration report
- Operator shift roster
Process steps:
1. Verify containment seal integrity and log baseline pressure.
2. Run control-system self-test and confirm no fault codes.
3. Ramp coolant pumps to nominal flow and monitor vibration.
4. Raise reactor output in 5% increments while recording telemetry.
Outputs:
- Stable thermal output at target setpoint
- Shift handoff log with anomaly notes
Constraints:
- Abort if vibration exceeds threshold.
Validation criteria:
- Output remains within tolerance for 30 minutes.
