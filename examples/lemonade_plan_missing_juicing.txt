Title: Lemonade Plan

Goal:
Make fresh lemonade for a summer picnic.

Ingredients:
- 8 lemons
- 1 cup sugar
- 6 cups cold water
- ice

Expected Output:
A pitcher of fresh lemonade.

Plan:
1. Wash the lemons and set them on the counter.
2. Add the sugar to a pitcher.
3. Pour in the water.
4. Stir until the sugar dissolves.
5. Add ice.
6. Chill the pitcher for 30 minutes.
7. Serve the lemonade to guests.

Notes:
The plan should be simple and easy to follow.
